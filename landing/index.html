QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently asked questions

How does NatePay work?

Create a payment link, share it with your client or supporter. They enter their card details and choose a billing date. That's it — you get paid automatically every month. No invoicing, no reminders, no chasing.

What are the fees?

We charge 4% per successful transaction. No monthly fees, no setup fees, no hidden costs. You only pay when you get paid. Stripe processing fees are included.

Which countries do you support?

We accept payments from 150+ countries. For payouts, we currently support Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and all Stripe-supported countries. Your subscribers can pay with any major credit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.

How do I get my money?

Money is automatically deposited to your local bank account. For Nigeria, Ghana, and Kenya, we convert and send directly to your bank. For other countries, payouts go through Stripe to your connected bank account.

What is income verification?

We generate official pay stubs and income statements from your NatePay earnings. These documents are formatted like traditional employment records — perfect for bank loans, apartment applications, or visa requirements.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There's no contract, no commitment. You can stop using NatePay whenever you want. Your subscribers can also cancel their subscriptions at any time — we believe in keeping things fair for everyone.
