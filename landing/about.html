THE PROBLEM

The "Cash & Chase" Crisis

There are two ways African service providers lose money today.

1. The Global Gap: Freelancers working for US/UK clients lose 15-25% of earnings to wire fees, bad exchange rates, and delays. 16% never get paid at all.

2. The Local Trap: Gyms, tutors, and estate managers operate on a "Cash & Chase" model. Because mobile money was built for one-time transfers, not subscriptions, business owners spend 30% of their time as debt collectors.

"We have 150 members, but every month feels like starting from zero. I spend more time chasing renewals than training clients."
